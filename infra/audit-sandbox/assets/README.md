# Cognito Managed Login Branding Assets

These placeholder images are required by the `actions-aws-auth` workflow. Replace them with your own branding:

- **background.png** – Page background (PNG, JPG, JPEG, or SVG; max 2MB)
- **logo.png** – Form logo (PNG, JPG, JPEG, or SVG; max 2MB)
- **favicon.png** – Favicon (ICO or PNG; max 2MB)

Supported formats and size limits are enforced by AWS Cognito Managed Login.
